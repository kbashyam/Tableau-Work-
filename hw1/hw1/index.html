Question 2: What Are The Most Recurring Crimes ??

For question #2, I added the filter to UCR dimension in the dataset. I filtered out something the author classified as part 3 in the ucr rating. They mainly consisted of calls made to investigate a suspicious person or a call made when someone was injured and needed medical assistance. My main consideration were crime that could harm an individual like a part1 or part2 crime ( FBI Ucr ).

Question 3: Identify a Trend For When Crime Is The Highest.

For question #3, I identified that the months may through september experienceed the most crime. This was attributed to the fact that these month experienced zero snowfall. While the time of day spiked at 5pm and 6pm typically after office hours get over. A "Part 3 Ucr" indicates a call made due to a an individual requiring assistance, so given a part2 or part1 crime most of them were called in within an hour.
